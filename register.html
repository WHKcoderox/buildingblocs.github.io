Register

Registration starts on 14 March 2017 (Pi Day!) and ends on 19 May 2017. BuildingBloCS conference is open to O-level Computing students and teachers only. If you are not the intended target audience but have a reason to attend, please email buildingblocs@computing.sg.

Register within 3 days from Pi Day inclusive and stand a chance to win a Raspberry Pi 3!. Sign up early and earn bonus lucky draw chances for extra tech swags. Lucky draw results will be announced during the conference on 1 and 2 June 2017.

Pi Day puzzle (adds to lucky bonus chance): What is the 10-digit integer whose digits are all distinct and the number formed by the first i of them is divisible by i for each i from 1 to 10? Email your Python program to buildingblocs@computing.sg. Happy coding!
